AprilTag Scenario Cards

This is a working example using AprilTag to pick a scenario card!

Click on the 'Toggle Webcam' button to hide/show the webcam for testing purposes.

Heavy Storm
Zones 6, 7, 10, 11 generate no revenue next cycle due to high density impact.
Wind Event
Zones 6, 7, 10, 11 cannot build anything due to high density impact.
Superstorm Event
Zones 1, 2, 4, 5, 8 lose 5 blocks of residential buildings.
Immediate Relief Fund
No tokens generated this cycle as all are used for immediate relief
Financial Crisis /div>
All players lose half their tokens
Resource Allocation Dilemma
o Players collectively lose 10 tokens. Each player must contribute at least 1 token
Infrastructure Failure
All inland zones lose all public services
Infrastructure Failure 02
All shore zones lose all public services.
Power Outage
All inland zones lose all commercial revenue for this round
Disease Outbreak
All inland zones lose all revenue for this cycle
Disease Outbreak
o All shore zones lose all revenue for this cycle
Public Health Crisis
Zones 6, 10, and 13 generate no revenue for the next three rounds and lose one residential building each
Political Unrest
The next player skips their turn due to citywide protests
Riots and Unrest
Department of Sanitation and Department of City Planning skip their next turn
Political Turmoil
EDC and Department of Transportation skip their next two turns
Toxic Spill
All Green Infrastructure in Zone 12 is contaminated and unusable. Players must use actions to clean up (demolish and rebuild) the spill before building new infrastructure
Contaminated Water Supply
Zones 2, 6, and 12 face water contamination issues. All public services in these zones become non-functional for the next three rounds.
Wildfire
Zones 3, 5, and 12 have all parks and residential buildings destroyed. Players must rebuild these zones completely.
Wall Failure
A wall barrier fails. Draw an additional flood card for this zone and spend 3 tokens to repair the wall, or the zone remains unprotected.
Emergency Wall Construction
Players must collectively build a wall barrier in Zone 2 within two rounds. If not completed, draw an additional flood card for Zone 2 each round until it is built.
Reinforcement Required
Players must reinforce an existing wall in Zone 9 by spending 2 tokens per player. If not reinforced, the wall will breach, and the zone will flood.
Economic Downturn
All players lose 3 tokens due to a sudden economic downturn affecting the city’s revenue.
Tax Increase
Players must pay an additional 2 tokens each due to a new tax increase. Failure to do so results in a penalty of losing one action this round.
Infrastructure Audit
Conduct an audit of your infrastructure. If any player’s infrastructure is found lacking, they must spend 5 tokens to upgrade it, or face reduced effectiveness in their zones.
Environmental Hazard
A hazardous material spill occurs in Zone 3. Players must spend 4 tokens to clean it up or face reduced revenue from this zone for the next two rounds.
Climate Change Initiative
Players receive 3 tokens each for investing in climate change mitigation efforts. However, zones with high pollution levels will experience reduced income this cycle.
Wildlife Conservation
A new wildlife conservation area is set up in Zone 5. All players must contribute 2 tokens each to maintain this area or suffer a penalty of losing 1 token per round until contributions are made.
Transport Strike
A transport strike disrupts deliveries. All players lose 2 tokens and face delays in infrastructure projects for the next round.
Supply Chain Issue
Due to a supply chain issue, players must spend 3 tokens to procure necessary materials. Failure to do so results in halted construction projects for this round.
Logistics Improvement
Players receive a bonus of 2 tokens each due to improvements in logistics. Additionally, all infrastructure projects proceed faster this round.
Community Rally
A community rally boosts morale and funding. All players receive 5 tokens and gain an extra action this round.
Public Support Boost
Public support increases. Each player receives 3 additional tokens and has reduced costs for infrastructure upgrades this round.
Volunteer Program
A new volunteer program is initiated. All players gain 2 tokens and can use one free action this round to complete any ongoing projects.
Tech Upgrade
A technology upgrade provides all players with 4 tokens each and speeds up construction times for this round.
Infrastructure Enhancement
Enhance your infrastructure by spending 5 tokens to improve efficiency. If not done, expect a decrease in zone productivity this round.
Energy Efficiency
Adopt energy-efficient practices. All players save 2 tokens on utilities and experience improved zone performance for the next two rounds.
Health Crisis
A health crisis impacts Zone 8. All players must spend 4 tokens to address health concerns or face reduced revenue from this zone for the next round.
Pandemic Response
A pandemic response initiative costs 5 tokens per player. Failure to participate results in losing 2 tokens each round until resolved.
Medical Facility Upgrade
Upgrade medical facilities in Zone 4. Spend 3 tokens to improve healthcare services or face higher costs and decreased productivity in this zone.
Public Safety Drill
Conduct a public safety drill. All players gain 2 tokens and are better prepared for emergencies this round.
Emergency Preparedness
Invest in emergency preparedness. Spend 3 tokens to ensure better response times or face a penalty of losing 1 token each round for unpreparedness.
Safety Equipment Upgrade
Upgrade safety equipment in all zones. Players spend 4 tokens each to enhance safety measures or face increased risks and potential damage.
Crisis Management
Implement crisis management strategies. All players receive 3 tokens and gain immunity from one random disaster this round.
Disaster Relief Fund
Allocate funds to disaster relief. Players must collectively spend 10 tokens or face additional penalties and reduced revenue in the next round.
Risk Assessment
Conduct a risk assessment. Each player spends 2 tokens to mitigate potential risks and avoid a penalty of losing 3 tokens next round.
