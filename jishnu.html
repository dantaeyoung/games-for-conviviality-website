Reflection: Cooperative Decision Making Process

But is it just a little bit more housing? Many would argue otherwise. The 'City of Yes Housing Opportunity' has sparked significant concerns and doubts among various communities, with some actively advocating against it. Residents worry about potential overdevelopment, changes in neighborhood character, and strain on local infrastructure. Despite its aim to address the housing crisis, the proposal faces strong opposition from those who fear its impact on their communities.
